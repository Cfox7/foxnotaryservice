Services

General notarizations for affidavits, powers of attorney, contracts, acknowledgements, and more. We verify identity, witness signatures if required, and provide an official stamp.

We come to your home, office, or hospital. Travel fee applies; please confirm location and number of documents when booking. ID and original documents are required.

We notarize POA documents and can advise on signature blocks and witness requirements. Some jurisdictions require specific wording — please bring a final signed copy.

Administration of oaths and sworn statements. We ensure the signer understands the statement and signs in our presence; certified copies and stamps provided as needed.

Remote Online Notarizations (where allowed). Requires an internet-connected device, valid photo ID, and a webcam/microphone. Additional identity verification fees may apply.

I can act as the employer's authorized representative for Form I-9 Section 2: I will examine the employee's original identity and work authorization documents and complete the employer-signed portion. Note: the employer remains legally responsible for the accuracy and completion of the Form I-9; I do not provide legal advice. Please confirm employer instructions prior to booking and bring original documents (no photocopies).

I assist with car title transfers and related notarizations. I can witness signatures, and notarize required documents. Please bring the signed title, valid photo ID for all parties, and any required forms from the Motor Vehicle Administration. I do not act as legal counsel; check MVA guidance for specific requirements before your appointment.
